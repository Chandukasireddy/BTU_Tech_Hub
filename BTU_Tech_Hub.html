Our Core Tracks

Everything we do is focused on two high-impact areas designed to provide maximum value and tangible outcomes for our members.

Track 1: Projects & Collaboration

Oct 15 - Oct 29

Phase 1: GenAI Sprint Kick-off

We'll launch our 6-week flagship project: building a Multi-Modal BTU Assistant using Gemini/LLaMA. Teams of 4-5 members will be formed to tackle this challenge.

Oct 30 - Nov 15

Phase 2: Core Development

Weekly check-ins focusing on industry-standard Git workflows and basic MLOps concepts, such as deploying a model via a simple FastAPI endpoint.

Nov 16 - Dec 5

Phase 3: Testing & Refinement

Engage in peer code review sessions to improve quality. The focus shifts to optimization, documentation, and creating a professional GitHub Readme for the project.

Dec 13

Project Demo Day

Teams will showcase their functional assistant tool to the group. A friendly panel will provide feedback on technical elegance and utility.

Track 2: Career & Skill Cohorts (4 Sessions)

Oct 25

Session 1: The Impact Resume

A hands-on workshop on writing powerful, metrics-based resume bullet points using the "Accomplished [X] as measured by [Y] by doing [Z]" framework.

Nov 8

Session 2: Portfolio & Application Strategy

Learn to set up a compelling GitHub profile and portfolio, plus practical tips for tailoring applications (cover letters, screening) for German tech companies.

Nov 22

Session 3: Technical Interview Prep

Participate in mock interview sessions focused on clearly explaining your project experience and foundational concepts like OOPS and Data Structures.

Dec 6

Session 4: Networking & Industry Connect

A dedicated session on professional networking, connecting with working professionals (including BTU alumni), and structuring mentorship outreach via LinkedIn.

Our Core Team Structure

We operate with an agile core team to ensure we stay focused on our mission and responsive to our members' needs.

Lead Organizer

Sets the overall vision, handles scheduling and external communication, and ensures all activities align with our mission.

Projects & Research Lead

Scopes and mentors our collaborative tech projects, and facilitates research discussion groups.

Career Development Lead

Designs and executes all career-focused cohorts, including resume workshops and mock interviews.

Outreach & Platform Lead

Manages our digital community, creates marketing materials, and handles member onboarding.
